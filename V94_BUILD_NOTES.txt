How to wire into index.html:
Add this script before your main app script or near the bottom before </body>:

<script src="quantum-brain.js"></script>

Then you can call:

RollingWrenchQuantumBrain.quantumSelfRepair()

And for quote messages:

RollingWrenchQuantumBrain.buildCustomerQuoteMessage(quote)
